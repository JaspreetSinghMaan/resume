ABOUT

I am working as a Project Officer at ACTDPL. I have submitted my PhD thesis in Computer Vision and Machine Learning at the Department of Computer Science, Punjabi University, Patiala. I have been working on desiging the equivariant/invariant neural networks for the task of image categorization. My research interests include computer vision, equivariant neural networks, image processing, computation neuroscienc and philosophy.
