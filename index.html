ABOUT

Presently, I am working as a Post Doctoral Research Associate (PDRA) in 3D Computer Vision and Deep Learning at the Lincoln Institute of Agri-Food Technology, University of Lincoln , UK under UKRI Project Titled: From Nitrogen Use Efficiency To Farm Profitability (NUE-Profits). I worked as a Project Officer at ACTDPL, Punjabi University, Patiala, India from 2022-2023.. I have finished my PhD in Computer Vision and Machine Learning at the Department of Computer Science, Punjabi University, Patiala, India. I have been working on desiging the equivariant/invariant neural networks for various computer vision tasks (classification, regression, segmentation, etc.). My research interests include 2D/3D computer vision, reconstruction, equivariant/invariant neural networks, image processing, and philosophy.
